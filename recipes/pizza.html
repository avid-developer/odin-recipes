Pizza

Description

Welcome to the realm of homemade pizza, where the aroma of freshly baked crust mingles with the tantalizing scent of bubbling cheese and savory toppings. In this recipe, we'll embark on a culinary journey to create a masterpiece that rivals your favorite pizzeria's offerings. From the satisfying stretch of dough to the artful arrangement of toppings, each step is infused with passion and precision, resulting in a pie that's as beautiful as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5-10 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, stirring until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let rise for 1-2 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread marinara sauce over the dough, then top with cheese and assorted toppings.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and enjoy!
