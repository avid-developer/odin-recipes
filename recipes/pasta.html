Pasta

Description

Enter the enchanting world of pasta, where strands of dough weave tales of tradition, comfort, and culinary creativity. In this recipe, we'll embark on a gastronomic journey to craft a dish that's as satisfying to the soul as it is to the palate. From the rhythmic swirl of fork against plate to the aromatic embrace of sauce and cheese, each bite is a celebration of simple yet sublime flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5-10 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, and stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Punch down the dough, then roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  5. Transfer the dough to a greased baking sheet, then top with marinara sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice, serve, and enjoy!
